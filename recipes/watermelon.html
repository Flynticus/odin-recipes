Watermelon: But Spicy?

Description

Did you wake up this morning and forget that you are invited to a 4th of July picnic? Are you expected to bring a dish and don't want to show up empty handed? We got your back! Try this take on satisfying watermelon. This watermelon has all the juciness you would expect of a watermelon with some heat behind it. Don't worry, it is oddly delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1/2 lime, juiced

Steps

  1. Mix all of the spices into a bowl
  2. Sprinkle small amount of spice mixture into bottom of selected single size serving dish
  3. Place desired amount of watermelon into the dish
  4. Sprinkle more of the seasoning on top to desired amount
  5. Add lime juice over the watermelon
