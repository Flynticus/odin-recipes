The All American Burger!

Description

Imagine being at a cookout. Coolers with drinks, music playing, smoke is puffing out of the grill with the scent of delicious meats being cooked. What are you grabbing for your first plate? How about a delicious All American Burger!

Ingredients

This is all relative, because you really just need ground beef and good ole American cheese. I will provide a list below, but feel free to customize!

  • 1.5lb of ground beef (80/20 is best for maximum flavor)
  • 3/4 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of bread crumbs
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • Hamburger buns

Steps

Note: If you want a more well done burger, cook for 4 minutes each side instead of 3.

  1. Turn on grill and set to medium heat and close the lid. Combine beef, garlic salt, black pepper, salt, and bread crumbs in a large bowl and mix thouroghly
  2. Form the beef into 4 equal size patties
  3. Place the beef patties on the now heated grill and close the lid
  4. Let the patties cook for about 3 minutes, flip the patties over, and close the lid
  5. Allow the patties to continue cooking for 3 minutes
  6. Open the lid and place 1 slice of American cheese per patty, then close the lid and turn off the grill if there are no other items to cook
  7. After 30 seconds, remove the patties from the grill and place onto your bun.
  8. Top it with your favorite condiments and enjoy!
